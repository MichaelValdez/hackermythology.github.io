DUST

“Dust thou art, and unto dust thou shalt return” (Genesis 3:19) I’ve always been fond of this particular phrase, even though it’s biblical. It was the first thing I could think of when I was watching the dust lying on my bedroom floor one night. It felt odd to focus on something that didn’t seem like the most obvious thing to turn your attention to.

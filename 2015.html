Performing ‘Semolina’, a sound piece for ‘Acousmonium’ at Concertgebouw Brugge

Acousmonium
Concertgebouw Brugge invited Esther Venrooij and young artists of LUCA Visual Art Gent and atelier Image Dans le Milieu ÉSAVL Liège, to focus on experienicing a space through listening. They will present their sound works on the Acousmonium, a loudspeaker orchestra designed in 1974 by French composer François Bayle. This orchestra consists of 24 to 100 loudspeakers of different size and shape, which will surround the audience. The artists will place and move the sounds in space, using a mixer, creating an auditive space.

Concert Acousmonium with works by Esther Venrooy and young artists of atelier Mixed Media LUCA (Beeldende Kunst Gent) and atelier Image Dans le Milieu® ÉSAVL (Académie Royale des Beaux-Arts de Liège).
